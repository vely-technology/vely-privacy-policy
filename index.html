Introduction

Welcome to Vely ("we," "our," or "us"). Vely is a couple-focused social networking application designed to help couples connect with other couples for friendships and social experiences.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully. By using Vely, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use Vely, you may provide us with:

  • Account Information: Email address, password
  • Profile Information: Names/nicknames, ages, gender identities, city/location, bio/description
  • Partner Information: Partner's nickname, age, gender (for couple profiles)
  • Relationship Details: Relationship status, years together, relationship type
  • Photos: Profile photos, verification photos
  • Preferences: Interests, lifestyle preferences, what you're looking for
  • Communications: Messages exchanged with other users

2. Automatically Collected Information

Data Type Purpose
Device Information App optimization, security, troubleshooting
Location Data (GPS) Show nearby profiles, distance-based matching
Usage Analytics Improve app features and user experience
Log Data Security, debugging, service improvement

3. Camera & Photo Access

We request camera and photo library access to:

  • Upload profile photos
  • Complete photo verification for account safety
  • Share images in chat conversations

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Our Services: Create and manage your account, display your profile to other users
  • Enable Matching: Show you relevant profiles based on location, preferences, and interests
  • Facilitate Communication: Enable messaging between matched users
  • Ensure Safety: Photo verification, prevent fraud, enforce community guidelines
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Process Payments: Handle subscription and premium feature purchases securely
  • Send Notifications: Matches, messages, and important account updates
  • Comply with Law: Respond to legal requests and prevent harm

How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

Your profile information (name, age, photos, bio, interests) is visible to other Vely users based on your preferences and matching criteria.

With Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party services for:

  • Supabase: Secure database and file storage
  • Payment Processors: Stripe and Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • Cloud Services: Infrastructure and hosting
  • Analytics: Understanding app usage (anonymized data)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Vely, our users, or the public.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in encrypted databases
  • Authentication: JWT-based secure authentication
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Manage notification preferences
  • Location: Disable location access in device settings
  • Block Users: Block and report inappropriate users

To exercise these rights, contact us at vely.adm1@gmail.com

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. When you delete your account:

  • Profile data is deleted within 30 days
  • Photos are removed from storage within 30 days
  • Message history is anonymized
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (up to 90 days)

Age Restrictions

Vely is intended for users 18 years of age and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately delete their account and associated data.

If you believe a minor is using our service, please report it to vely.adm1@gmail.com

International Users

Vely operates from India. If you access our services from outside India, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India or other countries where our service providers operate.

By using Vely, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.
