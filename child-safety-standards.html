Vely Dating App - Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

1. Age Restriction Policy

Vely is exclusively designed for adults aged 18 years and older. We strictly prohibit the use of our app by minors.

  • Users must be 18+ to create an account
  • Date of birth verification required during registration
  • Accounts are automatically rejected for users under 18
  • Age is displayed on all user profiles

2. Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) Prevention

Vely has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation material.

  • Prohibited Content: Any content involving minors in sexual or suggestive contexts is strictly forbidden
  • AI Photo Verification: All profile photos undergo verification to ensure authenticity
  • Content Moderation: Automated and manual review of user-generated content
  • Immediate Action: Accounts found violating CSAM policies are permanently banned

3. User Reporting System

We empower our community to report inappropriate content and behavior.

  • In-App Reporting: Users can report profiles, photos, and messages
  • Report Categories: Includes "Underage User" and "Inappropriate Content"
  • Confidential Reporting: Reports are private and secure
  • 24-Hour Review: All reports reviewed within 24 hours

4. Safety Features

  • Block Functionality: Users can block unwanted contacts
  • Profile Verification: AI-powered photo verification system
  • Content Filtering: Automated detection of inappropriate content
  • Secure Messaging: End-to-end encrypted communications

5. Cooperation with Authorities

Vely cooperates fully with law enforcement regarding child safety:

  • Immediate reporting of CSAM to National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Compliance with all legal reporting requirements
  • Preservation of evidence for law enforcement investigations
  • Cooperation with international child protection agencies

6. Parental Controls

While Vely is for adults only, we support device-level parental controls:

  • Rated Mature 17+ / Adults 18+ in app stores
  • Compatible with iOS Screen Time and Android Digital Wellbeing restrictions
  • Age-gated content in app store listings

7. Contact for Child Safety Concerns

To report child safety concerns:

  • Email: support@velyapp.com
  • Subject Line: "Child Safety Report - URGENT"
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours for child safety reports

For emergencies, please also contact local law enforcement and:

  • US: National Center for Missing & Exploited Children - CyberTipline.org
  • International: Contact your local child protection authorities

8. Employee Training

All Vely team members receive training on:

  • Recognizing signs of child exploitation
  • CSAM reporting procedures
  • Platform safety policies
  • Legal compliance requirements

9. Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and update our child safety measures:

  • Annual safety audits
  • Technology updates for better detection
  • Community feedback integration
  • Compliance with evolving regulations

Vely is committed to maintaining a safe, adult-only platform. We have zero tolerance for any form of child exploitation and work diligently to protect minors from accessing our service.
