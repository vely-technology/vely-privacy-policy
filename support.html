Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a couple profile?
When setting up your profile, you'll have the option to add your partner's information. Both of you will appear together in your couple profile visible to other users.
How do I verify my profile?
Go to your profile settings and select "Verify Profile." You'll be asked to take a selfie in a specific pose to confirm your identity. Verified profiles get a badge and are shown more prominently.
How does matching work?
Swipe right on profiles you're interested in. When both couples like each other, it's a match! You can then start chatting and plan to meet.
How do I change my location?
Your location is automatically detected using GPS. To update it, go to Settings > Location and tap "Update Location." Premium users can change their location to any city.
Can I block or report someone?
Yes! Tap the menu icon (‚ãÆ) on any profile or conversation and select "Block" or "Report." Blocked users cannot see your profile or contact you.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your app store:
  • Android: Google Play Store > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions
  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account in the app: Profile > Settings > Delete Account. For more details, visit our Account Deletion page.
Is my data safe?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption for all data. Your photos are stored securely, and we never sell your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Safety Tips

Your safety is important to us. Here are some tips for using Vely safely:

  • Protect your information: Never share your password, financial information, or home address with other users.
  • Video chat first: Consider video chatting before meeting in person to verify identities.
  • Meet in public: For first meetings, choose busy, public places and let friends know your plans.
  • Trust your instincts: If something feels wrong, it probably is. Don't hesitate to block and report suspicious users.
  • Report suspicious behavior: Help keep our community safe by reporting profiles that seem fake or users who behave inappropriately.
